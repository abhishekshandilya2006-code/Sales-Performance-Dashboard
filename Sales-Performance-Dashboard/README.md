***# Sales Performance Dashboard***



***This project is an interactive Sales Performance Dashboard developed in \*\*Power BI\*\* to analyze business sales data and generate meaningful insights. The dashboard helps track overall sales, profit, customer trends, product performance, and payment preferences through interactive visualizations.***



***## Project Highlights***



***- Built an interactive dashboard using Power BI.***

***- Created KPI cards to monitor Total Sales, Profit, Quantity, and Average Order Value.***

***- Used slicers for quarterly analysis.***

***- Designed charts to compare sales across states, customers, categories, and payment modes.***

***- Applied DAX measures and Power Query for data transformation and calculations.***



***## Dashboard KPIs***



***- \*\*Total Sales:\*\* 438K***

***- \*\*Total Profit:\*\* 37K***

***- \*\*Total Quantity Sold:\*\* 5615***

***- \*\*Average Order Value:\*\* 121K***



***## Dashboard Insights***



***- Maharashtra recorded the highest sales among all states.***

***- Clothing was the top-selling product category by quantity.***

***- Cash on Delivery (COD) was the most frequently used payment method.***

***- Printers generated the highest profit among all sub-categories.***

***- Profit showed a noticeable decline during July and December.***
